# Task 03: Hero Section with Gradient & CTA

## Introduction
The "Hero Section" is the most important part of a landing page. It's where you convert visitors into users.

## Learning Points
- **Background Gradients**: Using `linear-gradient` to create depth and modern aesthetics.
- **Button Hover Effects**: Using `transition` and `transform: translateY` to make buttons feel interactive.
- **Text Overlay**: Ensuring text is readable over backgrounds.
- **Centering Techniques**: Using Flexbox (`justify-content` and `align-items`) to perfectly center content in the middle of the screen.

## Why this matters for SaaS
Every SaaS needs a landing page. A high-quality hero section makes your product look professional and trustworthy.

## How to run
1. Open `index.html`.
2. Hover over the buttons to see the animation.
3. Observe how the background color changes from one corner to another.
